Frequently Asked Questions

Why does my team need a data catalog?

According to Forrester, data catalogs enable:

  • $2.7 million in time saved due to shortened data discovery
  • Increased self-service use of data by business users
  • $286,085 savings from shortening new analyst onboarding
What makes Datalogz better than other data catalogs?

A problem common to almost every company is that data knowledge lives with only a small group of superusers. Our unique value lies in the creative ways Datalogz extracts this information to drive data democratization and productivity across your entire team.

How can Datalogz help as my team migrates to the cloud?

As you migrate to the cloud, user adoption is always a priority. Datalogz can increase cloud adoption by enhancing the data consumer experience and prioritizing data movement. What needs to be migrated? What data is not being used? What does this data even mean? All of these questions can be answered with Datalogz.

How can Datalogz help as my company grows?

As companies grow, data can quickly become a black hole. Analysts lose comprehension of what the data means and data becomes a messy world. Datalogz fixes this by ensuring proper discovery, documentation, and context every step of the way.
