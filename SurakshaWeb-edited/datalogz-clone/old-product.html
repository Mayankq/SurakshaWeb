How we do it

Datalogz uniquely creates BI governance through three pillars:

The Safety Net

  • Continuous monitoring for cost optimization, security risks, and reporting risks

Data Enablement

  • Discover datasets and reports with context 
  • Automate 70% of metadata creation 
  • Feedback loop to source business metadata

BI User Help

  • Analyze data quality with custom business quality tests 
  • Optimize refresh and sync schedules 
  • Alerts around data changes in the BI layer
